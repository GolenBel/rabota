Interesting anime Facts:

"Death Note"

1. In the original manga, Light Yagami was introduced as a father of a family with his wife and children. However, in the anime, this concept was changed to make the character more lonely.
2. The voice of Light Yagami in the Japanese version of the anime is voiced by Mamoru Miyano, who also voices Levi Ackerman in Attack of the Titans.
3. The 3 scenes in which Ryuk writes in a Death Note were created using real footage of writing upside down.
4. Ryuk's 4 eyes were modeled after the eyes of a lizard, and his name comes from the Japanese word "fish".
5. The number of victims of Light Yagami was reduced in the anime compared to the manga.
6. The names of the characters from The Death Note are often referred to famous criminals. For example, the name Light Yagami is similar to the name of serial killer Richard Ramirez, and the name L resembles the name of the famous detective Hercule Poirot.

"Attack of the Titans"

1. The idea of creating "Attack of the Titans" came to Hajime Isayama during a visit to an amusement park, where he was struck by the idea of people running from giant humanoid creatures.
2. Animation studio WIT Studio initially refused to film the manga due to its violent content.
3. The main character Eren Jaeger was named after a character from a Turkish novel, and his surname refers to the German word "huntsman", meaning hunter.
4. The sound effects used for the Titans' screams were created by recording the cries of babies and other non-human sounds.
5. The world of "Attack of the Titans" is inspired by medieval Germany, and in particular the city of Nordlingen.
6. Paradise Island is based on the Japanese island of Kyushu.
7. The walls protecting the island are named after the Tokyo districts: Sina, Rosa and Maria.
8. Titans are inspired by Japanese mythology, in particular the legends of oni.
