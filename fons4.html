"The Alchemist"

1. In the original manga, Salim Bradley was a homunculus named Pride, but this fact went unnoticed in the first anime due to interruptions in the release of new chapters of the manga.
2. In the first anime, Hohenheim leaves his family so that they do not watch his death, and in the new anime he is immortal and leaves in search of a way to die.
3. The world of the "Steel Alchemist" is made in the entourage of the early XX century.
4. Characters who have studied chemistry and physics in depth are proficient in alchemy.
5. Alchemists are forbidden to create gold and human organisms.
6. Alchemists can transform substances, create chimeras and infuse souls, but obey the law of equivalent exchange.
7. Bestowal is an effect that occurs when an alchemist tries to get more than he gives.
8. For transmutation, it is required to draw an alchemical circle and activate it.
9. Alchemists who come into contact with the Truth can transmute without a circle. The Xin Empire uses alcohol.
10. The plot of the 2009 manga and anime features an Immortal army - an army of homunculi created to conquer the world.
