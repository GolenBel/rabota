5. Ranks in the "Blade cleaving demons".
The demon slayers in "Blade Cleaving Demons" are divided into 10 ranks.

Mizunoto -> Mizunoe -> Kanoto -> Canoe -> Tsuchinoto -> Tsuchinoe -> Hinoto -> Hinoe -> Kinoto -> Kinoe
And all these levels use "concentrated breathing" inspired by the medical technique of "box breathing".

Boxing Breathing Practice:
1. Take a deep breath through your nose, counting to 4.
2. Hold your breath, counting to 4.
3. Exhale slowly through your mouth, counting to 4.
4. Hold your breath, counting to 4.
5. Repeat the cycle for 5 minutes.
Additionally:
Increase the time of each stage to 8 seconds.
Practice in a calm environment.

6. Muzan can not only read the thoughts of his demons.
In the anime, additional panels from the manga were removed, namely the narrator's comments.
They specified that everything that the demon sees or hears is transmitted to the Muzan. He can also share his memories with demons and track their location.
Tamayo, Yushiro and Nezuko were able to lift this curse.

7. Tanziro and his abilities.
Tanziro is a talented swordsman who can use two breathing styles: water and solar.
He cares and empathizes with demons and their stories, which causes sympathy. By the way, the initially faceless mangaka Koeharu Gotoge made him cold-blooded, but changed his mind in time.
Tanziro also has a weakness for smell and can sense other people's emotions.
And in the video game "Demon Slayer", which was released in 2021, there are three techniques that are not mentioned in the anime. The key "Phoenix Flash".

THE MEANING OF THE NAME :
Tan - coal. That's what he was doing at the beginning of the story.
Ji - to heal. Probably a hint of Nezuko.
Ro is the sun. "The Breath of the Sun."

9. Sanemi - marechi.
Marechi are people with the rarest blood, which is the most beloved delicacy for demons and seems to fascinate them only with its smell. Intoxicating makes you more vulnerable. This fact helped the boy in hunting demons.
Muzan and Sanemi also weigh the same and have the same height (177.8 cm).

10. Gemei is the strongest of the Hashiro clan.
Gemei is blind, which explains the reason for the lack of pupils in his eyes. He was blinded by a fever that he had as a child.
Gemei loves cats and playing the flute. One day he played for so long that his grandmother hit him with a broom.
